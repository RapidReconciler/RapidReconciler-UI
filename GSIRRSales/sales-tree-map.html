Patterns & observations

  • The chart is now the page's master index. The sales-client-management page used to have three module sections below the chart (onboarding, documents, collateral). All three are gone. Their content is now reached via the topnav, the hero collateral buttons, the doc tray, or by clicking a step in the chart and reading the sidebar.
  • Three audiences, three pieces of collateral, no overlap. Battlecard for the rep (internal). Self-Guided Tour for the curious prospect (early). Fact Sheet for the technical evaluator. The Overview piece used to overlap with the Fact Sheet's executive job; it's been retired.
  • Aesthetic stratification is intentional. Battlecard uses utility design (IBM Plex). Self-Guided Tour uses one marketing identity (DM Sans / Instrument Serif). Fact Sheet uses another (Barlow). Internal workflow pages use Source Sans 3. Each piece's design signals its audience.
  • SOW and MSA are no longer orphans. Both templates are now referenced from the Process Documents tray and from inside the relevant step pane (2A and 5). They retain their hub link as the secondary pathway.
  • One non-obvious cross-folder bridge exists. ../GSIRRTech/start-here-monitor.html links into rr-installation-prep.html from the tech side. The sales side does not return the favor — no sales page references anything in tech.
  • Most playbook and template pages still don't back-link to the workflow root. Once you're on a playbook page, the only path back to sales-client-management.html is the browser back button. This is a content-design decision that has not been changed by the rebuild — but it remains the largest navigation gap in the corpus.
