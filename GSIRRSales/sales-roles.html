Customer Documents & Templates

Documents and contract templates that go to customers, prospects, and auditors. Two kinds: documents are pre-built artifacts emailed out on request — compliance attestations from GSI and static reference guides from sales (link or attachment, depending on sensitivity); sales templates are fillable forms that sales completes in the browser, prints to PDF, and uploads to DocuSign.

Documents

Compliance attestations & sales reference guides · SOC 2 period: Apr 1, 2024 – Mar 31, 2025

SOC 2 Bridge Letter

Link OK
1 page · updated annually · covers gap to current date

Single-page attestation from GSI confirming no material changes in the control environment between the SOC 2 Type 2 audit period end and the current date. Customers ask for this when their own audit period extends past the SOC 2 report’s coverage end-date. Lower-sensitivity than the full report — safe to share via direct link.

SOC 2 Type 2 Report

Attach manually
46 pages · current period: Apr 1, 2024 – Mar 31, 2025

Full audit report from UHY LLP. Restricted-use document — per the report’s own language, intended only for GSI, existing user entities, business partners, prospective user entities and partners, and regulators. Do not share via direct link. Verify recipient legitimacy first, then attach the PDF to the email.

Before sending: confirm the recipient has signed an NDA, is an existing user entity, or is a regulator with a stated need. Then click Compose, attach SOC2-Type2-Report.pdf from the Compliance folder, and send.

Sales Templates

Owned by Sales · fill in browser, save as PDF, upload to DocuSign

Master Services Agreement

Fillable form
9 fields · 33 sections · auto-save · DocuSign-ready

Use for services engagements — ongoing consulting, technical support, managed services, or anything where statements of work hang off the master agreement. SOWs are separate documents that reference back to this MSA. No order form is included — that lives in each SOW. Fill the highlighted fields, click Print / Save as PDF, then upload to DocuSign.

Open template →

Software License Agreement

Fillable form
Includes Order Form · auto-save · DocuSign-ready

Use for software licensing deals — the customer is buying RapidReconciler licenses with an attached order form covering quantity, fees, maintenance, and consulting rates. One-shot transaction (not a framework for ongoing services). The order form is built in — no separate SOW needed. Fill the highlighted fields, click Print / Save as PDF, then upload to DocuSign.

Statement of Work

Fillable form
Up to 4 task line items · auto-totalled · auto-save · DocuSign-ready

Use for discrete consulting engagements under an existing MSA — database migrations, server reconfigurations, scoped technical work. References the MSA by date and inherits its terms. Includes Attachment A (scope) and Attachment B (fixed-fee task table with up to four line items). Fill the highlighted fields, click Print / Save as PDF, then upload to DocuSign.
