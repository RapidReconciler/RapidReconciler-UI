InternalRapidReconciler · Sales Battlecard
v · May 2026 · 1 of 1

SaaS for JD Edwards. Replaces month-end inventory reconciliation in Excel.

RapidReconciler is a SaaS solution for JDE customers who reconcile perpetual inventory, in-transit, and Received Not Vouchered to the GL. It runs nightly, breaks variance into six labeled sources, and surfaces JDE configuration issues before they compound into period-end variances. Think of it as automated 3-point reconciliation plus 11 integrity checks plus an audit trail — replacing what most teams do by hand in spreadsheets.

Who to sell to JDE customers, manufacturing & distribution
  • JD Edwards World or EnterpriseOne customer (any release)
  • Uses the Inventory or Purchasing modules
  • Manufacturing or distribution business
  • Reconciliation taking days per period, not hours
  • Audit pressure — internal, external, or both
  • Reliance on a single person to close the books
Disqualifiers: Municipalities (historically not a fit). Companies without active inventory or purchasing modules. Pure services businesses.
Pain triggers — what you'll hear them say
  • We close every period in Excel.
  • Only one person on the team really understands the reconciliation.
  • The auditors flagged it last year.
  • We carry a variance forward every month and we don't know why.
  • It takes us a week to figure out what changed.
  • We just did a JDE upgrade and the numbers stopped tying out.
  • Our reports take too long to run.
  • We can't get to root cause without tracing each transaction by hand.

Fast answers if they ask, you have it
How long does implementation take?
Typically a 2-hour web meeting after the customer's server is ready. Fully operational within 3 days, guaranteed.
Is it on-prem or cloud?
SaaS, hosted in Microsoft Azure. Inventory and transaction data stays in the customer's JDE system.
Is it secure? Do you have an audit?
SOC 2 Type 2 audited by UHY LLP, period covers Apr 2024 – Mar 2025. Bridge letter available. Report under NDA.
What JDE versions are supported?
All JD Edwards EnterpriseOne releases. JDE World is also supported.
What database platforms?
Oracle, IBM i (AS/400), and Microsoft SQL Server.
Multi-company / multi-currency?
Yes. Filter by base currency, company, business unit, object, and subsidiary. Multi-company is standard.

What's the license model?
Per JD Edwards company number — typically one license per company. Customer provides company numbers at provisioning.
How is data extracted from JDE?
Read-only via SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS). No writes to JDE. Nightly refresh is standard.

TBD — RR is JDE-focused; native integrations with other systems should be confirmed case-by-case.
Audit / SOX support?
Built-in Audit Report (PDF or Excel). Used directly by customer auditors. As-Of reporting for prior-period validation.
What if the customer's JDE has heavy customization?
Flag during discovery — custom programs hitting F4111/F41021 can affect setup. Loop in RR DBA early.
