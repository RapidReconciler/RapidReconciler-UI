How to use this page
⚠ About these analyses — verify before acting

The analyzer surfaces likely root causes based on patterns it recognizes today. Customer configurations and uncommon transaction types may produce findings that don’t fit your situation. Always verify the diagnosis against your JDE setup before posting correcting entries or changing configuration.

If a finding looks wrong, fill in the Notes tab in the analysis workbook and email it to rrsupport@getgsi.com — we’ll review and expand the pattern library.

For a full walkthrough of how to use this page, see the How to use the Export Analyzer guide.
