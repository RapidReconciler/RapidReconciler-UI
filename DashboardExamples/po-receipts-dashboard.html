About This Report
PO Receipts tracks inventory and service purchases where the receipt has been posted but the supplier invoice (voucher) has not yet been matched. The gap between Received Not Vouchered (RNV) and the GL must reach zero before period close. The $54K Rec vs GL variance shown here represents this accumulated RNV balance across 228 active suppliers. Supplier names anonymized for display.
