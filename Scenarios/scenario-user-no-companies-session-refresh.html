Blank or missing data

User can’t see newly granted companies

Your admin granted you access to additional companies, but you don’t see them in the company selector. Your browser session is holding the old, narrower permission set. A widget refresh or a fresh login almost always fixes it.

What you’re seeing

  • Your admin granted you access to one or more companies, but when you click into a tab (Reconciliation, Inventory, etc.) you see no companies in the company selector.
  • Your account is active and your admin confirms your access is saved correctly.
  • Common phrasings: “I can’t see my new company,” “my newly granted companies aren’t showing,” “the company dropdown is missing options I should have.”

Try these steps in order

Step 1
Ask your admin to confirm your access was saved correctly
When this applies: always check this first — it’s the simplest cause.

If the Authorized Companies dialog was closed with the X instead of Save, the changes can be silently dropped — the admin thinks they saved your access, but the change didn’t persist. Ask your admin to verify:

  1. Open Admin → Users and click the edit pencil icon for your account.
  2. Confirm the right companies are ticked under the column that matches the tab you’re trying to use (Reconciliation has its own column, Inventory has its own, etc.).
  3. If any boxes are unticked, tick them and click Save (not the X).
Saved correctly? Continue to Step 2.
Step 2
Refresh the company selector widget

Your session is most likely holding the old permission set. The widget has a refresh that pulls the latest security from the server.

  1. On the page you’re on, click the refresh icon in the top-right corner of the company selector / widget filter area.
  2. Check whether the missing companies appear.
Resolved? Stop here. Otherwise, continue to Step 3.
Step 3
Log out and back in

A fresh login pulls the current security settings from the server and bypasses anything the browser cached for the previous session.

  1. Log out of RapidReconciler completely.
  2. Log back in.
  3. Open the affected tab and check the company selector again.
Resolved? Stop here. Otherwise, continue to Step 4.
Step 4
Hard-refresh the browser, then log in again

This clears any cached page assets that might still be holding old permissions.

  1. On the affected page, press Shift+F5 on Windows or Cmd+Shift+R on Mac.
  2. Log in again and check the company selector.

If you still see no companies, ask your admin to re-open your user record one more time to verify the boxes are still checked — closing the dialog with X instead of Save can drop the changes silently.

Resolved? Stop here. Otherwise, continue below to email RR support.
