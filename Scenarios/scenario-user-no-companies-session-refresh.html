Blank or missing data

User can’t see newly granted companies

Your admin granted you access to additional companies, but you don’t see them in the company selector. Your browser session is still holding the old, narrower permission set — logging out and back in, then refreshing the widget, picks up the new access.

What you’re seeing

  • Your admin granted you access to one or more companies, but when you click into a tab (Reconciliation, Inventory, etc.) you see no companies in the company selector.
  • Common phrasings: “I can’t see my new company,” “my newly granted companies aren’t showing,” “the company dropdown is missing options I should have.”

Try these steps in order

Step 1
Log out and back in

A fresh login pulls your current security settings from the server and clears whatever the previous session was holding.

  • Log out of RapidReconciler completely.
  • Log back in.
Continue to Step 2 to refresh the widget.
Step 2
Refresh the company selector widget

Click the refresh icon in the top-right corner of the company selector / widget filter area on the affected tab. The newly granted companies should appear.

Resolved? Stop here. Otherwise, continue below to email RR support.
