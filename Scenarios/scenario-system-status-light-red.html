Status lights red

System status light is red

The System Status indicator at the top of the Reconciliation page has turned red. This signals that a backend job has crashed — usually because of disk space or a data-processing error. There’s a built-in export that captures the crash details, and that’s the key piece of information to send to support.

Different cause family: if the red status light is paired with a Cardex variance that won’t clear after month-end and a missing future period, see Cardex variance and red status light after month-end. That’s a data-duplication issue your IT can address — different from the click-and-email-Excel path below.

What you’re seeing

  • The System Status indicator at the top of the Reconciliation page has turned red.
  • Common phrasings: “system status is red,” “the red light is on,” “the job failed,” or “something crashed.”
  • Reports or scheduled processing didn’t complete the way they normally do.

Try these steps in order

Step 1
Capture the basic context

Note the following before you do anything else — this becomes the email if support needs to investigate.

  • The date and time the indicator turned red.
  • The company or business unit you were viewing.
  • A screenshot of the Reconciliation page header showing the red System Status indicator.
Captured? Continue to Step 2.
Step 2
Click the red System Status indicator to export the crash details

Clicking the red indicator exports an Excel report with the crash details — this is the artifact RR support needs to diagnose what went wrong.

  1. Click the red System Status indicator at the top of the Reconciliation page.
  2. Save the Excel file that downloads.

If clicking the indicator doesn’t do anything, capture a screenshot of the indicator and any browser console errors instead and note that the export failed.

Got the Excel? Continue below to email RR support.

Email RapidReconciler support

Send the crash details

A red System Status almost always needs RR support to read the Excel and route it to the right person. Email rrsupport@getgsi.com and include:

  • The Excel report exported from clicking the red indicator (the most important attachment).
  • Your screenshot of the Reconciliation page header with the red indicator visible.
  • The date, time, and company / business unit from Step 1.
  • If the Excel export didn’t work, say so and include a screenshot of any browser console errors.

Email rrsupport@getgsi.com
