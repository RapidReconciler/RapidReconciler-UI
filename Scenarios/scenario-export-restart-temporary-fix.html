Scenario · Application server · export job

Daily export fails — "restart service" is a temporary fix — works some days, not others; restart helps for ~24 hours.

Customer reports the End-of-Day export works some days and not others. Restarting the RapidReconciler service on the app server is a workaround that lasts about 24 hours. The "restart fixes it for a day" pattern is the diagnostic gold — recommend a full app-server reboot for a more durable fix.

Section 1

Scenario description — what the customer is reporting.

Symptom

Customer reports the End-of-Day export works some days and not others. They have already discovered that clicking restart service on the RR app server resolves it temporarily, but it stops working again within ~24 hours.

Symptom variants that fold into this scenario:

  • Customer phrases it as "the export sometimes doesn't work" or "we've been restarting the service every day"
  • They may have asked their internal IT to handle the daily restart but want a more permanent fix
Section 2

Possible solutions — ordered roughly easiest to hardest.

1 Recommend a full application-server reboot Path 2 · Respond directly
When this applies: The "restart service is a temporary fix" pattern is the diagnostic gold — it indicates the underlying service is in a bad state that a full reboot will clear. The customer's own IT can execute the reboot once you recommend it.
Steps to resolve
  1. Recognize the "restart service is a temporary fix" pattern in the customer's description — this is the key signal.
  2. Reply to the customer recommending a full application-server reboot rather than the daily service restart. Frame it as "the service restart is treating the symptom; a full reboot should clear the underlying state and give a more durable fix."
  3. Note that this requires their IT to schedule a brief outage on the app server. Suggest off-hours timing.
  4. Ask them to confirm when the reboot is scheduled, then to update the ticket after it's been done.
  5. Follow up after 48 hours to confirm the export is now stable. If the issue recurs after a full reboot, escalate to RR Network Tech — the workaround/restart pattern means there's a deeper issue worth investigating.
Real example
Beta Industries — Tier 1 had the answer

Beta Industries reported the daily export was failing intermittently. The "restart service is a temporary fix" detail in their email was the diagnostic gold. The Tier 1 helpdesk reply — "recommend a full app-server reboot" — turned out to be the right answer; Beta's own IT team executed the reboot and the export stabilized.

Provisionally resolved with monitoring. The pattern is now codified in this scenario for future inbox triage.
