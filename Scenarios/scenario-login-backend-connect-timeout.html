I can’t log in

Several things can prevent you from logging in to RapidReconciler. The login screen usually shows a note that identifies which one. Match what you’re seeing to the right option below.

What you’re seeing

  • You can’t sign in to RapidReconciler — the login form rejects your attempt.
  • The login screen shows a note explaining the reason: password expired, incorrect credentials, server unavailable, or a generic connection error.
  • Common phrasings: “it won’t let me in,” “I can’t logon,” “I forgot my password,” or “the server’s down.”

Match the message and follow the path

Option 1
Password expired or incorrect credentials
When this applies: the login note says your password is expired, or your credentials are incorrect.

RapidReconciler passwords must be reset every 90 days. Whether yours expired or you simply don’t remember it, the resolution is the same:

  • Click the Forgot Password link on the login screen and follow the reset flow.
  • If the Forgot Password flow doesn’t reach you (no reset email arrives, or the reset link doesn’t work), email your RR Administrator and ask them to help.

Option 2
Any other login error For your IT
When this applies: the login screen shows anything other than a credentials issue — a connectivity error, “server unavailable,” or any error message you don’t recognise.

You won’t be able to fix this from the login screen yourself — it’s a workstation, network, or server issue your IT needs to look at.

  • Take a screenshot of the error message on the login screen.
  • Email it to your IT using the button below. Drag the screenshot into the email before you send.
